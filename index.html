Fraction

A fraction represents a part of a whole.

A half, 1/2, is more than a quarter, 1/4!

Weekly Word

Character

A character is a person or being in a story.

Characters have traits that describe them.

Geography

  • Where is Ukraine?

Ukraine

Ukraine is in Eastern Europe.

It is bordered by Russia, Belarus, Poland, Slovakia, Hungary, Romania, and Moldova.

Presidents

  • first president?

First President

George Washington was the first president of the United States.

He served from 1789 to 1797.

Artist

  • Cézanne

Paul Cézanne

Paul Cézanne was a French artist and Post-Impressionist painter.

He laid the foundations for the transition from 19th-century art to a new and radically different world of art in the 20th century.

Money

  • exchange rate

Exchange Rate

An exchange rate is the value of one country's currency in relation to another currency.

For example, how many euros you can get for one US dollar.

Rugby

  • scrum

Scrum

A scrum is a way of restarting play in rugby.

Players pack closely together with their heads down and attempt to gain possession of the ball.

Calcium

  • milk, cheese

Calcium

Calcium is a mineral that helps build strong bones and teeth.

Good sources include dairy products like milk and cheese, as well as certain vegetables.
